Walk to Boemi Kedaton Mall from our hotel in Bandar Lampung

For easy access to universities, restaurants, and shopping outlets in Bandar Lampung, book a hotel room with pool or city views at Radisson Lampung Kedaton. You can easily walk from the hotel to Boemi Kedaton Mall, the biggest mall in the city, or to Lampung Museum, where you can learn about local history. If you’re up for an adventurous day trip, you can travel about three hours away to snorkel at Pahawang Island or to glimpse dolphins at Kiluan Bay.

When you return to the hotel after your excursions, unwind with a refreshing dip in our outdoor pool. You can also keep in shape at our well-equipped gym and connect with home or the office using free Wi-Fi. Need a lift to or from Radin Inten II International Airport (TKG)? We can arrange airport transfers for a fee.

Host up to 1,500 guests in our stylish meeting facilities

With 1,750 square meters of meeting space spread across five rooms and a ballroom, our hotel is perfect for a range of events. Our pillarless ballroom welcomes wedding receptions and large corporate conferences for up to 1,500 guests, while our smaller meeting rooms can accommodate more intimate team-building sessions and training seminars. For added convenience, our hotel also offers airport transfers to Radin Inten II International Airport for a fee.

Things to do in Bandar Lampung

Mutun Beach

9.16 mi / 14.74 km from the hotel

Escape to this tranquil beach that features calm seas, white sand, and huts for relaxing on the shoreline. More adventurous visitors can take advantage of boat and canoe rentals or hire an ATV vehicle to explore inland.

Puncak Mas

3.48 mi / 5.6 km from the hotel

For breathtaking views of Bandar Lampung, head to this attraction that overlooks the rolling hills of the city. Scale the wooden platform for panoramic views, or take a spot on a bench while the kids run around the playground.

Lembah Hijau

3.05 mi / 4.92 km from the hotel

Connect with nature at this family-focused park, home to horses, birds, and other animals. If you’re feeling adventurous, give the zip line a try or check out the water park. Spanning 30 hectares, the park offers something fun for everyone.

Lampung Museum

1.44 mi / 2.33 km from the hotel

Learn about Lampung’s history and its heroes at this museum that features thousands of artifacts, including beautiful Chinese ceramics.

Boemi Kedaton Mall

0 mi / 0.01 km from the hotel

Browse the stores at Lampung’s largest shopping mall, where you can pick up souvenirs from your trip or let the kids run around and climb at the on-site playground.

Al Furqon Grand Mosque

3.27 mi / 5.27 km from the hotel

The largest mosque in Bandar Lampung, this religious landmark offers a place to pray or to capture breathtaking photos.
